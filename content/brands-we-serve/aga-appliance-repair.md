## Certified AGA Appliance Technicians in Ontario, For Homes & Heritage Kitchens

AGA appliances have a long history of cast-iron construction, heat-retaining cooking systems, and unique ignition/ventilation requirements, especially older or heritage models. When an AGA oven fails, or a cast-iron range shows wear, you need technicians with deep experience in their specialized mechanics.

**{{< company-name bold >}}** provides expert **AGA appliance repair** across Ontario, servicing both modern AGA installations and vintage cast-iron ovens still in regular use.

We repair AGA ranges, ovens, cooktops, and hotplates in houses, heritage homes, cottages, and custom kitchens.

We handle:

- Classic cast-iron ovens and ranges
- Modern gas or electric AGA units (where installed)
- Cooktops and hotplates
- Maintenance and restoration of older ovens to preserve functionality

## Why Professional AGA Repair Matters

AGA appliances operate under higher heat retention, heavier components, and sometimes older internal systems. Incorrect repair can lead to poor heat distribution, safety hazards, or permanent damage to the cast-iron build.

Professional AGA repair ensures:

{{< list >}}
Accurate heat distribution in ovens and ranges
Proper ignition and safe operation of burners
Preservation of structural integrity and longevity of cast-iron units
Safe ventilation and flue operation where required
Reliable performance without frequent breakdowns
{{< /list >}}
