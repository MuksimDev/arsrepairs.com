## Why Professional Dacor Repair Matters

Dacor systems operate within tight tolerances. A weak igniter, drifting sensor, or failing control board can cause uneven cooking, error codes, or shutdowns. Ignoring early signs often leads to larger failures.

Professional Dacor repair helps:

{{< list >}}
Restore accurate heat and temperature control
Prevent damage to gas valves, heating elements, and boards
Correct airflow and ventilation issues
Reduce stress on motors, fans, and electronic components
Extend the lifespan of premium cooking and cooling appliances
{{< /list >}}

We test performance under normal use before completing the repair.
