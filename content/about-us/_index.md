## Who We Are

ARS Repair Inc. is an appliance repair and installation company serving homeowners, property managers, and commercial clients across Miami and South Florida. We focus on dependable service, practical solutions, and clear communication from the first call through completed repair.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to keep homes and businesses running smoothly by delivering professional appliance repair and installation services backed by transparent pricing and consistent workmanship.

## What Sets Us Apart

### Trained & Experienced Technicians
Our technicians are professionally trained and experienced with a wide range of residential and commercial appliances. ARS Repair Inc. is an authorized repair centre for select brands such as Samsung and LG, and our team is factory-trained on many other major brands commonly used in South Florida.

### Stocked Service Vans
Our service vehicles carry commonly used parts and tools, allowing many repairs to be completed without unnecessary delays or repeat visits.

### Transparent Pricing
We provide clear, flat-rate pricing before any work begins. You'll know what to expect before a repair is approved.

### 90-Day Workmanship Guarantee
All repairs are backed by a 90-day workmanship guarantee. If an issue relates directly to our repair, we'll address it at no additional cost.

### Local Dispatch & Support
Our dispatch team operates locally in South Florida, helping customers schedule service, receive updates, and make appointment adjustments when needed.

## Service Excellence

- Over 2,400 repairs completed in 2024

## Our Service Areas

ARS Repair Inc. proudly serves customers throughout:

## Commercial Appliance Services

In addition to residential service, we support commercial clients with:
- Priority service programs for restaurants and hospitality
- Property management and multi-unit appliance support
- Commercial kitchen equipment repair and maintenance
- Volume and account-based pricing options

## Licensed & Insured

ARS Repair Inc. is fully licensed and insured in Florida. All technicians are background-checked and trained to work safely in residential and commercial environments.
