faq:
  title: Frequently Asked Questions About Tracking Your Technician
  items:
  - question: How do I track my technician?
    answer: Enter your invoice number and house number in the form above, or click
      the secure tracking link in your confirmation email. When your visit is active,
      you'll see your technician's status, location, and estimated arrival time.
  - question: What will I see when I track my technician?
    answer: You'll see when your technician is on the way, their approximate location
      on the map, and the most up-to-date estimated time of arrival, so you know when
      to expect them.
  - question: When does technician tracking become available?
    answer: Technician tracking typically becomes available on the day of your scheduled
      appointment, once your job is assigned and your technician is en route. If you
      don't see tracking yet, check back closer to your appointment window.
  - question: Can I contact my technician directly from the tracking page?
    answer: If contact options are available, you'll see them on the tracking page.
      Otherwise, please call us at **866-415-3937** and our team will assist you.
  - question: What if I don't see my technician on the map?
    answer: Make sure you've entered the correct invoice number and house number,
      and refresh the page after a few minutes. If you still can't see your technician,
      call us at **866-415-3937** so we can check the status for you.
  - question: Is my technician tracking information secure?
    answer: Yes. Technician tracking is only accessible using your unique invoice
      number and house number or the secure link we send to your email. We protect
      your information and do not share it with third parties.
