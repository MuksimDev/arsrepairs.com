faq:
  title: Frequently Asked Questions About the Client Portal
  items:
  - question: How do I access the client portal?
    answer: You can access the client portal by entering your invoice number and house
      number in the forms above, or by clicking the link provided in your service
      confirmation email. The portal is available 24/7.
  - question: What can I do in the client portal?
    answer: The client portal allows you to track your repair status in real-time,
      view your technician's location and estimated arrival time, access your complete
      service history, download invoices, and manage your account information.
  - question: Do I need to create an account?
    answer: No account creation is required. Simply enter your invoice number and
      house number to access your service information.
  - question: Can I schedule new appointments through the portal?
    answer: To schedule new service, please use our online booking system or call
      **866-415-3937**.
  - question: How do I get my invoice number?
    answer: Your invoice number is provided at the completion of service, sent via
      email, and included on your service receipt. If you've lost it, call **866-415-3937**
      and we can help.
  - question: Is my information secure?
    answer: Yes. We take data security seriously. Your service information is protected
      and only accessible using your invoice number and house number. We do not share
      your information with third parties.
  - question: Can I schedule new appointments through the portal?
    answer: The portal is designed for tracking and account access. To schedule new
      service, please use our online booking system or call **866-415-3937**.
  - question: What is the service call fee?
    answer: The standard service call (diagnosis) fee is **$120 + tax**. This fee
      covers travel and assessment time and is non refundable. If you proceed with
      the repair, it is credited toward your final repair total.
  - question: What warranty do you offer on repairs?
    answer: ARS Repair and Installation Services Inc. provides a **90 day warranty
      on labour** and a **30 day warranty on replacement parts**, covering only the
      same issue that was repaired.
  - question: What does the warranty not cover?
    answer: The warranty does not cover unrelated issues, misuse, improper maintenance,
      external damage, pests, weather events, or appliances that have been relocated.
  - question: What is your cancellation policy?
    answer: Appointments must be cancelled at least **12 hours in advance**. A **$60**
      fee may apply for late cancellations, missed appointments, or if we cannot reach
      you at the scheduled service time.
  - question: Do you send text message updates?
    answer: If you opt in with a mobile number, we may send SMS messages related to
      scheduling updates, technician arrival notifications, and customer support.
      Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply **STOP** to
      opt out or **HELP** for assistance.
