Common Refrigerator Issues We Fix

Refrigerators are complex appliances that require regular maintenance and sometimes repairs. Some common refrigerator issues we handle include:

  • Fixing temperature problems : If your refrigerator isn’t cooling or is too cold, it could indicate issues with the thermostat, compressor, or evaporator fan. We can quickly diagnose and resolve these issues.
  • Ice maker problems : If your ice maker isn’t working or isn’t dispensing ice, we can fix it by checking for blockages, water line problems, or electrical faults.
  • Water dispenser malfunctions : A common issue in refrigerators is when the water dispenser stops working. This could be due to a frozen line, faulty valve, or electrical issue.
  • Condensation or leaks : Leaks or excess condensation inside or around the refrigerator often indicate problems with the door seals, the defrost system, or the drain system. We fix these problems by inspecting the fridge’s internal components and seals to ensure they are functioning properly.

When your broken refrigerator is causing trouble, don’t wait for the situation to worsen. Call ARS Appliance Repair Service, and we’ll get it working again in no time. We’ll ensure that your refrigerator is cooling, dispensing ice and water, and functioning efficiently.
